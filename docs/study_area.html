Rationale for Exclusions

Why These Areas Were Excluded

Non-Comparable Lighting Requirements

Different land uses have fundamentally different lighting needs:

  • Airports: FAA-regulated aviation lighting, not residential street lighting
  • Parks: Intentionally darker for ecological reasons; different safety standards
  • Industrial/Port: 24/7 operational lighting unrelated to residential equity
  • Institutional: Universities, hospitals have independent lighting systems
  • Water Bodies: No residential lighting needed

Including these areas would distort equity analysis by comparing residential neighborhoods to areas with incomparable lighting purposes.
